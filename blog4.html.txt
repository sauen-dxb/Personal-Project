        <h2>The Power of Lyrics and Sound in Expressing Pain</h2>
        <p>
            Music has a unique ability to evoke emotions, and artists like Billie Eilish and Lana Del Rey often tap into themes of sadness, loneliness, and despair. Through their lyrics and soundscapes, they create a world where listeners can find comfort in shared pain. However, when music continually focuses on themes of suffering, it can influence how listeners view their own emotions.
        </p>
        <h3>Striking a Balance Between Catharsis and Glamorization</h3>
        <p>
            While music can provide an important outlet for processing difficult emotions, there is a fine line between offering catharsis and glamorizing pain. Artists and audiences alike should consider how music's representation of mental health impacts listeners' well-being.
        </p>
